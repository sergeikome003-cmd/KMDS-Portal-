Standard KMDS Fees:

Last Mile Delivery Fee:
A delivery fee (port/warehouse to your final destination in Cameroon) also applies, starting from a minimum of 2,500 XAF for the smallest parcels. Larger, heavier, or bulkier items (appliances, furniture, bikes, etc.) cost more depending on weight, size, and dimensions. Your exact fee is confirmed once we have your item's details from the supplier and will be added to your final invoice.
